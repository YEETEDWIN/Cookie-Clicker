Explains what each button does

Buttons cost clicks to upgrade which you can earn by click the cookie!

+ 1 Auto-Clicker

The top button or +1 Auto-Clicker allows each second to get a automatic click. The higher the level the more clicks you get automatically each second

Cookie Lottery

The fourth button is a cookie lottery which gives you a chance to get from 0 to 3000 clicks. It can also give a negative amount of -3500

Cookie Packet Search

The fifth button is a cookie packet search where you look inside a cookie packet and have a chance to get a 500 clicks cheque. The biggest prize is a COOKIE which gives you 5000 clicks. You can also get infected COOKIE which makes you lose 3500. There is also Death which makes you lose 5000 clicks

Items that you can find (the brackets indicate the amount of clicks):

  1. Death (-5000)

  3. 500 Clicks Cheque (500)
  4. 1000 Clicks Cheque (100)
  5. Golden Ticket (2500)
  6. Cookie (5000)

If a button has lvl G instead of lvl 0 that means its a gambling button which means you can either lose money or win money.
